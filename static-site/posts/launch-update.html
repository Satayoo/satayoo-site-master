Launching the Satayoo Site

19 August 2025

Welcome to the first iteration of Satayoo’s online home! In this post we outline why we built this site, the phases of work we’ve planned and how you can follow along as we improve it.

Phase 1 focused on fixing the broken links and providing a proper navigation structure. We’ve created dedicated pages for patents, blog, docs and contact, added a real contact form and ensured our call‑to‑action buttons point somewhere meaningful. Future phases will add substantive content and interactive features.

Stay tuned for deeper dives into our technology, including our Rally AI patent and Agentic platform. We’re excited to share more details with you soon!
